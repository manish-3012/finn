Merge Two Sorted Linked List 🔗

Given a Linked List, return the data present in the middle of the linked list :)

Approach 1 (Naive)

One way to find the middle element is to count the total number of nodes. Once we got the total number of nodes (let's say n), after that we will simply traverse from the starting node (n/2) times. And at the end of traversing it for (n/2) times, we will be at the middle node of the linked list :)
