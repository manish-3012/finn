Sort an array of 0s, 1s and 2s

An array contains both positive and negative numbers in random order. Rearrange the array elements so that all negative numbers appear before all positive numbers.

Algorithm:

1. Simply point two pointers let's say Jay and Veeru. Jay will traverse through the array.
2. Whenever Jay come across any negative person, I mean negative element. Jay and Veeru will simply swap those persons, as they wanna bring positive person ahead of negative ones lol. So negative one will be sent to the position where Veeru is standing.
3. Once Jay has traversed through all the elements, all the negative elements would have come at the beginning :)
